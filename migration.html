Track the epic journeys of penguins across the globe!

Emperor Penguin Migration

Emperor penguins undertake incredible journeys to reach their breeding grounds. They travel hundreds of kilometers across the harsh Antarctic ice, facing blizzards and freezing temperatures.

Learn more about Emperor Penguin Migration

Adelie Penguin Migration

Adelie penguins also migrate to their breeding colonies, often traveling over sea ice. Their migration is timed to coincide with the availability of krill, their primary food source.

Learn more about Adelie Penguin Migration

Humboldt Penguin Migration

Humboldt penguins, unlike their Antarctic cousins, live in warmer climates. Their migration patterns are influenced by the El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO), which affects food availability.

Learn more about Humboldt Penguin Migration
